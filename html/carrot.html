Garden Guide for Zone 8b

Carrot

Daucus carota

Carrots are a popular root vegetable that are easy to grow in sandy soil. They are a cool season crop and can be planted in the spring and fall.

Quick Info

  • Soil: Sandy, well-drained soil
  • Water: Regular, do not let soil dry out
  • Light: Full sun
  • Sowing Method: Direct sow
  • Growing Days: 70

Planting Instructions

Planting Instructions: Sow carrot seeds directly in the ground. Space seeds 2 inches apart in rows 12 inches apart. Cover with 1/2 inch of soil and water well.

Harvest Instructions

Harvest Instructions: Harvest carrots when they are bright orange and the desired size. Gently pull from the ground or dig around the carrot with a garden fork if soil is compacted.
