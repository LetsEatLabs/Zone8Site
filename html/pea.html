Garden Guide for Zone 8b

Peas

Pisum sativum

Peas are a cool-season crop, grown for their sweet, round, edible seeds. They are a member of the legume family and are known for their ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Quick Info

  • Soil: Well-drained, rich in organic matter
  • Water: Regular, do not let soil dry out
  • Light: Full sun to partial shade
  • Sowing Method: Direct sow
  • Growing Days: 60

Planting Instructions

Planting Instructions: Sow seeds 1 inch deep and 2 inches apart in rows 18 inches apart. Provide a trellis or other support for climbing varieties.

Harvest Instructions

Harvest Instructions: Harvest when pods are full and peas are round. Regular harvesting encourages more production.
