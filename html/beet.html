Garden Guide for Zone 8b

Beets

Beta vulgaris

Beets are a root vegetable most commonly deep red or purple. They are a great source of fiber, folate (vitamin B9), manganese, potassium, iron, and vitamin C.

Quick Info

  • Soil: Well-drained, loamy soil
  • Water: Regular watering, do not let soil dry out
  • Light: Full sun to partial shade
  • Sowing Method: Direct sow
  • Growing Days: 55

Planting Instructions

Planting Instructions: Sow beet seeds 1/2 inch deep and about 1-2 inches apart. Thin the seedlings to 3-4 inches apart once they are 4-5 inches tall.

Harvest Instructions

Harvest Instructions: Harvest beets when they are about 1.5-3 inches in diameter. The greens can also be harvested and eaten.
