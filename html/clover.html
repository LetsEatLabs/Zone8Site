Clover

Trifolium

Clover is a small perennial plant in the legume family that is often used as a ground cover or forage crop. It has trifoliate leaves and small, typically pink or white, flowers.

Quick Info

  • Soil: Well-drained soil
  • Water: Moderate
  • Light: Full sun to partial shade
  • Sowing Method: Direct sow
  • Growing Days: 60

Planting Instructions

Planting Instructions: Sow clover seeds directly into the soil, about 1/4 inch deep. Space the seeds about 6 inches apart.

Harvest Instructions

Harvest Instructions: Harvest clover when the flowers are in full bloom for the best flavor and nutritional value.
