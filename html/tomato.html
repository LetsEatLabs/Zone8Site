Garden Guide for Zone 8b

Tomato

Solanum lycopersicum

Tomatoes are a popular garden plant due to their ease of growth and the versatility of the fruit in cooking. They are a warm-weather vegetable and come in many varieties, from small cherry tomatoes to large beefsteak tomatoes.

Quick Info

  • Soil: Well-drained, fertile soil
  • Water: Regular watering, do not let soil dry out
  • Light: Full sun
  • Sowing Method: Indoor sowing, then transplant outdoors
  • Growing Days: 60

Planting Instructions

Planting Instructions: Start seeds indoors 6-8 weeks before the last spring frost date. Transplant outdoors after danger of frost has passed and soil has warmed.

Harvest Instructions

Harvest Instructions: Harvest when fruit is fully colored and slightly soft to the touch.
