Watermelon

Citrullus lanatus

Watermelon is a scrambling and trailing vine in the flowering plant family Cucurbitaceae. The species originated in West Africa, and is cultivated for its fruit.

Quick Info

  • Soil: Sandy loam
  • Water: Regular watering
  • Light: Full sun
  • Sowing Method: Direct sow
  • Growing Days: 80

Planting Instructions

Planting Instructions: Sow seeds 1 inch deep in hills spaced 6 feet apart. Each hill should have 2-3 seeds.

Harvest Instructions

Harvest Instructions: Harvest when the fruit is fully grown and the underside has turned from white to creamy yellow.
